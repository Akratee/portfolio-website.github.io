Communication

Social media communication refers to the exchange of messages, ideas, opinions, and content through social networking platforms. It can take many forms — text, images, videos, emojis, live streams, comments, shares, and likes — allowing individuals and organizations to interact, influence, and build communities at scale.

Research

Social media research is the systematic study of how individuals, communities, organizations, and societies interact through digital platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter (X), TikTok, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and others. As social media continues to reshape communication, culture, and commerce, research in this field has become critical across disciplines .
